About Magical Survival Game

Magical Survival Game is a browser-based fantasy RPG where players explore a magical world and battle increasingly dangerous enemies. Players choose a character class (Mage or Swordsman) and use a mix of combat skills, magic, and items to survive encounters across multiple levels, from early foes such as Skulls to the final boss, the Dragon. Along the way, players can explore to earn coins, meet friends/NPCs who provide helpful abilities (such as healing or learning spells), and visit a shop to buy weapons, armour, and potions. Each player’s progress is saved to their account so they can return and continue their journey, and the game is won when the Dragon is defeated.

What you can do

  • Create an account and log in to save your progress.
  • Fight enemies using standard attacks, magic skills, and items.
  • Earn coins through battles, missions, and exploration.
  • Visit the Shop to buy weapons, armour, and potions.
  • Recruit Friends (Phoenix, Shooter, Fairy, Hulk) for special abilities.
